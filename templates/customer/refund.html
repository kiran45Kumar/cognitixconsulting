Refund Policy

Effective Date: 07/11/2024

At Cognitix Consulting, we strive to provide the best products and services to our customers. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

1. Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund under the following conditions:

Courses and Training & Consulting Services:

  • Refund requests must be made within 24 hours of purchase or 14 days before the course start date for a 100% refund, whichever is earlier.
  • 50% of the course fees will be deducted for refund requests made 4 days before the start date.
  • No refund if the request is raised 3 days before the start of the session.
  • If you have accessed more than 10% of the course content, you may not be eligible for a refund.

Digital Products (eBooks, eLearning, Templates, etc.):

  • Refunds are not available for digital downloads once accessed or downloaded unless the product is defective.

Live Sessions or Webinars:

  • No refunds will be provided for missed sessions.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at:

  • Email: finance@cognitixconsulting.com
  • Phone: +91-6360 997 345

Include the following details:

  • Full Name
  • Order Number
  • Reason for Refund Request

3. Refund Process

Once we receive your refund request, we will:

  • Review your request within 2 business days.
  • Notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method within 15 business days.

4. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund after the specified time:

  • Check your bank account again.
  • Contact your credit card company or payment provider; it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.
  • If you’ve done this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at finance@cognitixconsulting.com.

5. Non-Refundable Items/Services

Certain purchases may not be eligible for refunds, including:

  • Completed live training sessions
  • Custom consulting services
  • Subscription fees beyond the current billing cycle

6. Changes to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."

Once refund is processed it will be back to source within the 5-7 days
